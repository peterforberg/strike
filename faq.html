Will we go on strike?

Union leadership has not announced plans to hold a strike authorization vote (SAV). So, there are a few possibilities:

  • We agree to a contract by 12/31
  • We work without a contract after 12/31
  • Leadership extends the contract past 12/31
  • Leadership announces an SAV after 12/31
